The Problem

Students frequently visit the pantry seeking specific items, such as milk and eggs.
As the pantry's stock fluctuates mainly from donations, setting up a live web page showcasing current items would allow students to preview available offerings online prior to their visit.

Design Process

Homepage Mockup

    1. Place essential navigation links at the header for easy access.

    2. Feature an introductory video in the hero section to familiarize new shoppers with the pantry.

    3. Include a highly visible button that offers users direct access to the status page, recognizing it as the primary destination for most users.

    4. Emphasize the pantry's benefits and its positive impact.

    5. Add a footer containing essential links for convenient navigation.

The Solution

The solution to this challenge is to develop a real-time web page that displays the Spartan Food Pantry's current inventory. This will allow shoppers to check the availability of specific items online before visiting the pantry, ensuring a more efficient and informed experience.

    • Insights & What I Would Do Different

  • Prioritize technology research: We focused too much on aesthetics at the expense of functionality. I would dedicate more time to backend technology research to align better with our goals.
  • Use more Bootstrap: We recognize Bootstrap's utility and believe increasing its integration within the project would have yielded significant benefits.
  • Conduct a user survey: It would have been beneficial to gather input from shoppers beforehand, asking for their suggestions and desires for the pantry's web services.
